Our Story

Born in a small garage in 2018, our band started with a simple dream: to create music that moves people. What began as weekend jam sessions between four friends quickly evolved into something much bigger.

Our debut EP "First Light" caught the attention of music enthusiasts worldwide, propelling us from local venues to international stages. Today, we continue to push boundaries and explore new sonic territories.
